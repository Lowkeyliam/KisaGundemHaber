- **Performance**: Lighthouse score >90

- **PWA**: PWA checklist %100
- **Bundle size**: <500KB total
- **Load time**: <2s first paint

- **Daily Active Users**: Target 1000+
- **Session duration**: Target 5+ dakika
- **Bounce rate**: <30%

- **Haber güncellik**: <15 dakika delay
